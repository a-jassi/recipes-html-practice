Lasagna

Description

Traditional, full-flavoured lasagna with pasta sauce noodles, and three cheeses.

Ingredients

  • 1 (26 Ounce) jar Prego Traditional Italian Sauce
  • 6 each uncooked lasanga noodles
  • 1 (15 Ounce) container ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cupe grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Spread 1 Cup pasta sauce in 2-quart shallow baking dish
  2. Top with 2 uncooked noodles, ricotta cheese, 1 cup mozzarella cheese, Parmesan chees and 1 cup pasta sauce
  3. Top with 3 remaining uncooked noodles and remaining pasta sauce
  4. Cover
  5. Bake at 375 degrees Farenheit for 1 hour
  6. Top with remaining mozzarella cheese
  7. Let stand for 5 minutes
